Program
The Sheikh Khalifa Government Excellence Program aims to encourage excellence practices in the federal government by adopting principles and spreading awareness about excellence, and directing and developing the capabilities of government agencies through the exchange of knowledge and best practices that encourage innovative thinking.
  • About the program
Back to main menu
Prize
His Highness Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Vice President and Prime Minister of the UAE and Ruler of Dubai, may God protect him, launched on June 24, 2009 the Mohammed bin Rashid Award for Excellence in Governmental Performance, to be the highest award for government excellence on the The state level, which aims to enhance the role of employees and federal government agencies in serving and achieving happiness and quality of life for all segments of society
